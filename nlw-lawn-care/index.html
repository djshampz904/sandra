Welcome to Kiambethu Farm

Just a short drive from the heart of Nairobi, Kiambethu Farm at Limuru provides a tranquil insight into life on a settler farm

Our smart approach
  • Situated at 7,200 ft., Kiambethu was bought and farmed by AB McDonell in 1910.
  • He was a pioneer in the tea industry being one of the first to make and sell tea commercially in Kenya, now one of Kenya's largest exports.
  • We offer tours Thursday to Sunday each week starting at 11am - please go to "Book Now" to see our current availability.
